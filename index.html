Engineering Networks at Scale

I'm a Carrier-Grade Telecom & Security Engineer and Technical Lead specialising in the critical, invisible layer every authentication request, subscriber session, and compliance log passes through. If a network can't correctly identify, authorise, or log its subscribers — everything downstream fails.

I've built RADIUS and TACACS+ platforms handling millions of authentication requests daily, COA pipelines that modify live subscriber sessions without re-authentication, and Network Monitoring Systems giving operators real-time visibility across their entire infrastructure.

On the compliance side, I've delivered IP Log Management Systems production-ready — the infrastructure operators cannot legally run without, built to meet DoT/TRAI mandates from day one, not bolted on after. I work across the full stack — from protocol-level AAA and EPC architecture through BSS/OSS, all the way to VAPT-hardened AWS deployments. Most engineers live in one layer. I build the bridge between them.
